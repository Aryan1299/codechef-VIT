    DEVSOC'19

    DEVSOC was a weekend full of intense coding with unlimited pizzas, coffee and goodies. It was aimed at providing a platform for developers to implement their ideas, ideas that could save this crumbling humanity. The winning team bagged a prize money of 50k, followed by the first runner up getting 20k and the second getting 10k. Also there were three special mention prizes which comprised of goodie bags full of swags. From key chains to T shirts the bags had it all. Technical quizzes were conducted where every correct answer either won a UFO charger or an MI wristband.
